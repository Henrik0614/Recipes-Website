Ratatouille

Home

Ratatouille is an ancestral French recipe, it's a spring recipe to enjoy hot or even cold! This is up to you! It's a 100% vegan recipe and can be enjoyed by everyone!

Ingredients :

  • 2 Zucchini
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1 Eggplant
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • A bouquet garni
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Preparation

  1. Wash and cut the zucchini, eggplant, green and red peppers into medium-sized cubes. Cut the tomatoes into quarters and slice the onion.
  2. In a frying pan, pour a little olive oil and fry the various vegetables one after the other for 5 minutes until they brown. Start with the peppers, then the eggplant, the zucchini, and finally the onions and tomatoes, which you will cook together.
  3. After cooking the vegetables, add them all to the tomatoes and onions, reduce the heat, and stir. Add a generous bouquet garni of thyme, rosemary, and bay leaves, season with salt and pepper, then cover and simmer for 40 minutes, stirring regularly.
  4. About 10 minutes before the end of cooking, add the two large crushed garlic cloves and re-cover. Feel free to taste and season again to your liking.
  5. Enjoy with grilled or barbecued food.
