Tiramisu

Home

Tiramisu is an Italian dessert, made with mascarpone and coffee, it is a refined dessert and will satisfy your taste buds!

Ingredients :

  • 100g Brown Sugar
  • 24 Ladyfingers
  • 30g Cocoa Powder
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Sachet Vanilla Sugar
  • 250g Mascarpone
  • 50cl Black Coffee Unsweetened

Preparation

  1. Separate the egg whites from the yolks
  2. Mix the egg yolks with the brown sugar and vanilla sugar.
  3. Add the mascarpone with a whisk.
  4. Beat the egg whites until stiff and gently fold them into the previous mixture with a spatula. Set aside.
  5. Quickly soak the biscuits in the coffee before coating the bottom of the dish with them.
  6. Cover with a layer of mascarpone cream then repeat the operation, alternating layers of biscuits and cream, ending with the latter.
  7. Sprinkle with cocoa.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours then enjoy chilled.
